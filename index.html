About Model UN at NASA Space Apps 2025

The Model United Nations (MUN) at NASA Space Apps 2025 brings together passionate students and professionals to engage in diplomatic discussions on global space policy, international cooperation in space exploration, and sustainable development through space technology.

This year's theme, "Diplomacy in the Final Frontier", challenges participants to represent different nations and space agencies in simulated UN committees, debating critical issues in space governance, planetary protection, and international space law.

Event Format

In-person conference with committee sessions

Duration

3-day immersive diplomatic experience

Committees

UNOOSA, COPUOS, Security Council Space Agenda

Preparation

Background guides and position papers required

Participants will develop skills in public speaking, negotiation, research, and international diplomacy while addressing pressing challenges in space policy. The event features guest speakers from space agencies, diplomatic corps, and international organizations.
